Accessible NYC

About this App

Accessible NYC reports the outage status of elevators and escalators throughout the New York City subway system. All data is retrieved directly from the MTA.

Made to be Useful

This app was made to be useful to you. Feel free to contact me via Twitter with your feedback, feature requests, or just to say hi.
